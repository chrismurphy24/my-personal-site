Hi! My name is Chris
Welcome to my site

I'm a product manager with a passion for levering my technical, business, and scientific skills to drive development of innovative software that enables the cutting edge of the biotech industry.

While I don't get to write code anymore in my everyday job as a product manager, I still enjoy the feeling of writing code and making something come to life on my screen. I used Material design for my front end development, and django for my blog application (an ode to every basic blog tutorial out there, but I also find django's out-of-the-box ORM super convenient). I'm using NGINX as my web server and uWSGI as the interface between that and my django app. Probably overengineered, but this is just as much about me exploring and practicing with various technologies as it is about telling the world a bit about myself!

About Me

Like I said earlier, I'm a product manager primarly focused on building innovative software in the biotech and life science industries. But more importantly, before being a PM, I'm a husband to my talented wife, Brianna, a dog dad to my chihuahua mix Milo, and a Blue Devil and diehard Cameron Crazie.
